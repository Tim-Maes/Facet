# Support

Use the channel that best matches your question so maintainers can respond efficiently.

## Where to ask for help

- **GitHub Discussions**: Design questions, usage help, and open-ended ideas.
- **GitHub Issues**: Reproducible bugs and actionable feature requests.
- **Discord**: Community chat, quick questions, and feedback on examples.
- **Security reports**: Follow the private process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

## Before opening an issue

Please:

1. Read the [README](README.md) and linked documentation.
2. Search existing issues and discussions.
3. Prepare a minimal reproduction if you are reporting a bug.

## Response expectations

Facet is maintained by a small open source team. Response times can vary, but clear reports with reproductions and version details are much easier to triage quickly.
